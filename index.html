Key facts about deadlines
📅
Two annual cycles
Large producers report twice yearly (H1 and H2). Small producers report once annually in April.
⚠️
Missing deadlines
The Environment Agency actively cross-checks submissions. Late or missing data triggers regulator contact and potential enforcement action.
🌍
Nation data — new
Introduced from 2024 data onwards. Producers must attribute packaging data to England, Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland separately.
// 03 — Industry Data

UK Reported Packaging Data

UK-aggregated packaging placed on market (POM) from the NPWD RPD portal. Industry totals only — individual company data is not publicly disclosed.

Large + small producers · extracted 11 May 2026

Total reported POM fell 6.1% like-for-like from 2024 to 2025 — across every material. This may reflect genuine packaging reduction in response to EPR financial incentives, improved efficiency, or some underreporting as smaller producers adapt. The 2026 data will be the clearest signal yet.

Figures in thousands of tonnes. Source: NPWD RPD portal, extracted 11 May 2026.

Reported packaging by material — 2023, 2024 & 2025

All seven material categories across three years. Thousands of tonnes placed on market (POM).

2023 2024 2025
What this may mean: Total reported POM fell from 12.15M tonnes (2023) to 10.77M tonnes (2025) — a 6.1% like-for-like decline from 2024 to 2025 across every material category. This could reflect genuine packaging reduction driven by EPR financial incentives, improved packaging efficiency, or some underreporting by smaller producers still adapting. Fibre composite (−13.3%) and glass (−12.0%) saw the sharpest falls. The 2026 data will be the most important signal yet.

// 05 — Policy Watchlist

Developments Worth Monitoring

Evolving policy areas that could materially affect packaging obligations in the coming years.

🧪
Active

Modulated Fees & RAM

Now in force from 2026/27. RAM determines your fee band. First modulated invoices expected October 2026.

Watch for: RAM guidance updates, fee band adjustments, Year 2 invoice confirmation.
🫙
Monitor

Deposit Return Scheme

DRS progressing across all four UK nations. If not operational by 2028, PET, aluminium and steel drinks containers fall under full pEPR.

Watch for: DRS launch confirmations, container scope, EPR exclusion guidance.
🇪🇺
Monitor

EU PPWR

EU Packaging Regulation introduces stricter recyclability and reuse requirements. UK businesses selling into the EU face dual compliance obligations.

Watch for: PPWR implementation dates, UK divergence decisions.
🏷️
Upcoming

Consumer Labelling

Recycle / Do Not Recycle removed from main regulations October 2024. Remains a 2027 policy direction — not currently a legal requirement.

Watch for: Reinstatement via secondary legislation ahead of 2027 target.
♻️
Monitor

Recycling Target Increases

UK targets expected to rise. Most materials currently below target, creating upward fee pressure over time.

Watch for: Defra consultations on material-specific targets and PRN obligations.
🌍
Upcoming

Nation Data Expansion

Nation data obligations still bedding in. Requirements expected to expand as the scheme matures across all four nations.

Watch for: Expanded attribution requirements and enforcement approach.
